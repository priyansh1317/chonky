In Loving Memory of My Dog

Chonky

Forever in Our Hearts


Chonky, a cute little bundle of love and joy, was more than just a pet; he was a loyal friend, a source of comfort and happiness, and a cherished family member.

Chonky's Journey

He came into our life for a very short period of time but left and incredible mark in my heart. We rescued him at just a week or two old, and brought him home. He quickly filled our home with joy, energy and puppy kisses. He always wanted to be around with us, demanding our attention. Unfortunately, our time with Chonky was cut short due to a tragic accident.
